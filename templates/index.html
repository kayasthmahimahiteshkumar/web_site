About Us

At Cakery, we craft exquisite cakes that delight your taste buds and make every celebration special.

Welcome to Cakery, your destination for freshly baked delights that make every moment sweeter. We are a passionate team of bakers and artists who believe that cakes are not just desserts—they are experiences. From classic chocolate indulgence to trendy red velvet and creamy cheesecakes, each creation is crafted with love, premium ingredients, and a sprinkle of joy. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or a simple celebration, our goal is to make your occasion truly unforgettable with the perfect cake.

At Cakery, we believe every cake tells a story. Whether it's for a birthday, wedding, or a sweet treat just because — we craft every dessert with care, passion, and high-quality ingredients. Our team is dedicated to creating visually stunning and delicious cakes that leave lasting memories.

Our Menu

Chocolate Cake

Our Chocolate Cake is a rich, moist, and decadent dessert made with premium cocoa, layered with smooth chocolate ganache, and topped with elegant swirls of frosting. Whether for birthdays, celebrations, or late-night cravings, this timeless classic never fails to satisfy your sweet tooth. Every bite melts in your mouth, delivering the perfect balance of sweetness and indulgence.

Red Velvet Cake

Our Red Velvet Cake is a moist and tender cake with a subtle cocoa flavor and a striking deep red color. It's layered with creamy, tangy cream cheese frosting that perfectly balances sweetness and richness. This classic cake is a favorite for celebrations and is sure to delight with every bite.

Cheesecake

Our Cheesecake is a rich and creamy delight, made with smooth cream cheese on a buttery graham cracker crust. Each slice melts in your mouth with a perfect balance of sweetness and tang. Topped with fresh fruits or a drizzle of berry sauce, it's an elegant dessert ideal for any occasion.

Rassbery cake

Our Raspberry Cake is a delightful treat layered with soft vanilla or almond sponge and filled with luscious raspberry compote. The natural tartness of fresh raspberries pairs beautifully with smooth buttercream or cream cheese frosting, creating a perfect balance of fruity and sweet. It's a refreshing choice for summer parties, anniversaries, or anyone who loves berry-filled desserts.

Rainbow Cake

Our Rainbow Cake is a vibrant and cheerful dessert made with six soft, colorful layers stacked high and frosted with smooth vanilla buttercream. Each bite offers a burst of color and flavor, making it perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or simply bringing smiles. It’s not just a cake — it’s a party in every slice!

Mango-Rasmalai-Fusion-Cake

Our Mango Rasmalai Fusion Cake is a luxurious blend of traditional Indian flavors and modern cake artistry. Soft vanilla sponge is soaked in saffron and cardamom-infused milk, layered with creamy rasmalai filling, and topped with fresh mango pulp. Garnished with pistachios and edible silver, this cake delivers a royal dessert experience in every bite.
